SWARANJALI

Solo Indian

Music is an unparalleled way of expression. Music is a powerful instrument to convey oneâ€™s emotions, whether they are violent or calm, passionate or apathetic, un-yearning or abjurant. SWARANJALI is a classical and semi-classical Hindi solo singling competition which has two rounds.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1) Performance without any accompanists till elimination round.

2) Classical and semi-classical theme preferred.

3) The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

4) Judging Criteria will be quality of singing (sur, taal and lay), vocal range, song selection and the overall impact of the song.

5) There will be 2 rounds one prelims round and a final round.

PRELIMS

1)Prelims rounds will be of "Hindi Solo Singing".

2)Participants can perform on any song of their choice.

3)Time limit will be 2 to 3 minutes.

FINALS

1)Selected singers would be choosing their songs on chit-picking basis.

2)Chits will have the name of a singer and a movie.

3)Participant can choose either a song sung by the given singer or a song from the given movie.

4)Deduction of Points/Marks if singers opt to pick next chit.

5) Time limit will be 3 to 5 minutes (including set-up and clearance time).

MESCOLANZA

Medley

When a group of people sing together, they make a chorus. When birds do, it's more like a whole symphony orchestra. Here show your musical enchantment with perfect synchronization of musical notes.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1) There will be only one round.

2) The time limit will be 10 minutes (including sound check).

3) Team size can be a minimum of 4 and a maximum of 10 vocalists. Maximum of 3 accompanists are allowed.

4) A team member can sing and play an instrument as well. However, such a person would be counted as both vocalist and accompanist.

5) Participants need to come with their own instruments.

6) The team may sing more than one song within the time limit.

7) Any Indian language is allowed along with English as well.

8) The decision of the judges and the coordinators shall be final and binding in all cases.

ANTAKSHRI

You can open the brimming box of your favourite songs and movies with our exciting rounds. In this rendezvous of lyrics and tunes, be ready to memorize even the oldest songs youâ€™ve ever heard. So just come and enjoy with us in our fun-filled antakshari.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1) Complete 'mukhda' of the song should be correct then only points will be given.

2) There will be counting from 1 to 5 if a team start singing the song but not able to finish it.

3) The final decision shall be of event coordinators.

4) There will be a written music quiz from which 4 teams will be short-listed for the final rounds.

ROUNDS

1) In the 1st round, you will be given a word on which the first team has to sing then next team will pick a word from the first team and so on. For correct song +10 otherwise 0. This process will take place twice.

2) In 2nd round one of the members of each team will pick a chit, in which a song will be written and he or she has to draw something to make the other members guess the song (no letters can be written). For correct song +10 otherwise -5. (This will be a buzzer round) 50 seconds will be given for guessing the name then it will be passed to other teams.

3) In 3rd round name of 2 Bollywood actors/actresses will be given who have some family relations. The team has to sing a song of any one of them and also guess their family relation. For correct answer 10+10 otherwise -5 if you can't sing any song. This will be an audience around.

4) 4th round will be an audio round where tune of a song will be played and you have to start the song and pick up the lyrics from where the music stops. +20 for the correct answer and -10 for the wrong answer.

SUR SANGAM

Duet Singing

SUR SANGAM is a duet singing competition based on classical or non-classical themes where you can showcase the great sync in your singing.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1) Maximum time allowed for each performance will be 6 minutes (from the entry on stage till exit from the stage).

2) Both partners can be male or female, the duo is not constrained to be a male-female duo.

3) Performance can be classical, non-classical or western.

4) A participant can only be part of 1 duet.

5) Registration can also be done on the spot.

PRELIMS

1) The contestants will have to perform a Hindi song of their choice.

2) The participants will be tested on a sense of scale and beat.

3) Accompanists (instrumentalists) are allowed. Participants will have to get their own musical instruments.

4) No karaoke or pre-recorded music (keyboard rhythm or guitar loops) will be allowed.

5) Time Limit will be 1.5 minutes to 3 minutes.

FINALS

1) Those selected in the prelims will have to sing the song either sung by the same artist(s)/singer(s) of which they have performed in the prelims or a song featuring the same lead actor/actress of the song.

2) The time limit will be 5 to 6 minutes.

BEAT IT

RAP Battle

Vivacity invites the music lovers from every genre to showcase their talent in the western music. Be it Pop, Country, Rap, Hip-Hop, etc. Just grab the mic and change the world in your rhymes and rhythms. Just Beat It!

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1) No accompanists are allowed in the elimination round.

2) For the finals, only one accompanist is allowed, either to play acoustic guitar or to give backing vocals.

3) The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

4) Judging criteria will be the quality of singing, vocal range, clarity of lyrics and overall impact.

5) There will be 2 rounds one elimination round and a final round.

ELIMINATIONS

1) Participants can sing any song of their choice, in any genre or sub-genre of music except Rock and Metal. No accompanists are allowed.

2) The time limit will be 2 to 3 minutes.

FINALS

1) Participants have to sing a song of their choice where music may be in form of karaoke or a live acoustic guitar played by the participant himself.

2) The time limit will be 3 to 6 minutes.
