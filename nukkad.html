Nukkad Naatak

Nukkad Naatak

Bring up the sizzling topics from the news to public's attention, scream your lungs out and capture the spectators' minds. It's your choice for a voice for the topics ranging from Anna to Chameli to Suicides to Success! Your Streets, your Natak!

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1)Time Limit will be 20 minutes.

2)Judging Criteria: Clarity of message, Overall Impact, Creativity, Humour, Expressions

3)The Competition will be conducted in an open place and hence no microphones, Speakers, Music systems will be allowed or available.

4)Teams can sing/dance by themselves to create the necessary music or sound.

5)No other prop is allowed except a 'dhafli' or a plate to give the rhythm and a 'chunni'.

6)Obscenity-The judges can award negative points and even disqualify the entry if they find any act objectionable.

7)The decision of Judges will be final and binding on all teams.
