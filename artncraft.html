face painting

This is a face painting event. It's time to do some magical, colourful and exciting artworks on your friend's face and win prizes for your creativity.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1)Any number of entries can be registered from one college.

2)Participation in the team of two.

3)Registration can be done online as well as on the spot (on the spot registration should be done 1 hour prior to the event)

4)Registration will be on the basis of first come first serve.

5)Necessary materials will be provided.

6)Duration will be 1 hour.

7)Judging will be on the basis of your creativity, the message conveyed by it, the use of colours.

8)In case of any discrepancy, Judge's decision will be final.

Ignitious

(Lemon n Fire Painting)

Let's freshen up this Vivacity with lemons. Leave your sour impressions right here and take your prize home.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1)Each team will be provided with a sheet of paper and related inventories.

2)They are supposed to draw something related to a situation, mental state, or any other written lines given on the spot.

3)The drawing should be done using lemon juice. After the drawing part is done, they are supposed to provide gentle heating to their entries.

4)As the heating will progress, the lemon juice on the sheet will start turning brown. The entries can be given aesthetic improvement by the manner of heating as well.

5)The teams will have to give a written explanation of their artwork in almost 50 words. The judging will be based on drawing skills, explanation, and the overall outlook of the artwork.

6)Time Limit: 2 Hours.
