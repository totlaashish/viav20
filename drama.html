Rangshala

Stage Play

The stage is set. The lights fade in. Curtains are up, The actors get into the characters and here starts the show.Amidst the drum rolls, slaying swords, moving words, blazing lights, you have to capture the audience with your gestures so subtle and emotions so strong, definitely not as lovely as it sounds. If you are ready to portray someone who you are not with all the zeal you have, this is your chance at it. POUNCE!

RULES FOR THE EVENT

PRELIMS

1) Each team will have to go through 10 minutes preliminary round in which they have to perform the main part of the skit which gives a brief idea about the play.

2) Also, teams need to submit a copy of their scripts beforehand, at least 3 hours before the competition.

MAINS

1) The time limit will be 30 minutes. Participants will be penalized by judges for exceeding the time limit.

2) Judging criteria: Acting, story, overall impact, use of the stage.

3) Medium: Hindi or English or both

4) All clothes accessories are to be arranged by the participating team.

5) The organizing committee is responsible only for the infrastructural facilities.

6) Obscenity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification

7) The decision of the judges will be Final and binding.

8) Participants must bring the background music for the skit in the standard secondary storage device.

Mono Acting

Have you of dreamt of going up on stage and delivering a splendid performance. If yes, then it’s time to make this dream a reality. The Mono acting competition gives you the liberty to present an acting piece of your choice, so get ready to leave judges astonished by your act.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1) Time Limit will be 5 minutes (Participant will be penalized for exceeding time limit)

2) Judging criteria will be Acting and Overall Impact.

3) This is a solo event.

4) Medium can be Hindi or English

5) Pre-recorded music is allowed and should be brought in a standard secondary storage device.

6) Pre Recorded music shouldn't contain any kind of narrations or voice over.

7) All clothes or props are to be arranged by the participating team.

8) The organizing committee is responsible only for the infrastructural facilities.

9) Obscenity (at the discretion of the judges) of any kind is not allowed and will lead to immediate disqualification.

10) The decision of Judges will be final and binding on all teams.

Mukh Abhinaya

Have you of dreamt of going up on stage and delivering a splendid performance. If yes, then it’s time to make this dream a reality. The Mono acting competition gives you the liberty to present an acting piece of your choice, so get ready to leave judges astonished by your act.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1)Complete 'mukhda' of the song should be correct then only points will be given.

2)There will be counting from 1 to 5 if a team start singing the song but not able to finish it.

3)The final decision shall be of event coordinators.

4)There will be a written music quiz from which 4 teams will be short-listed for the final rounds.

ROUNDS

1) In the 1st round, you will be given a word on which the first team has to sing then next team will pick a word from the first team and so on. For correct song +10 otherwise 0. This process will take place twice.

2) In 2nd round one of the members of each team will pick a chit, in which a song will be written and he or she has to draw something to make the other members guess the song (no letters can be written). For correct song +10 otherwise -5. (This will be a buzzer round) 50 seconds will be given for guessing the name then it will be passed to other teams.

3) In 3rd round name of 2 Bollywood actors/actresses will be given who have some family relations. The team has to sing a song of any one of them and also guess their family relation. For correct answer 10+10 otherwise -5 if you can't sing any song. This will be an audience around.

4) 4th round will be an audio round where tune of a song will be played and you have to start the song and pick up the lyrics from where the music stops. +20 for the correct answer and -10 for the wrong answer.
