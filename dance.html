MUDRA

Solo Indian

Let the mudras speak for you; Let the rhythm carry you away... Do you think you can captivate the audience with your grace? Here is your chance! Face the audience alone, and feel the music in your heart resonate to the beats, a dancer, thou art!

RULES FOR THE EVENT

PRELIMS

1) Prepared sequence.

2) The time limit will minimum 1.5 to maximum 3 minutes (including the setup time).

3) Only classical and semi-classical forms of dance allowed.

4) The time limit should not be exceeded.

5) Get two copies of your song one in a CD and one in a pen drive.

NOTE: Songs should be in .mp3 format and no audio cassettes will be entertained.

6) The song should be reported one hour prior to the PRELIMS.

FINALS

1) The participant must bring their own props and costumes.

2) The time limit will minimum 4 to maximum 6 minutes (including the setup time).

3) Props involving fire are strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.

4) The time limit should not be exceeded.

5) Decisions of organizing committee and judge will be final.

NOTE: Use of any props which is dangerous to you or others around you is strictly prohibited

LET'S TANGLE

Duet Dance

Be it salsa, samba or hip-hop show us the twists and moves! Put your best foot forward and mesmerize the audience with your sizzling chemistry and innovative props, a deadly combination to conquer the stage. In this ultimate face-off, prove that your pair is the best.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1) The competition is open only to college students with a valid college ID card.

2) Participants must perform on Bollywood songs only.

3) The time limit will be 4 to 6 minutes.

4) Props are allowed (Participants should bring their own props).

5) Any kind of fluid, flame and knives are not allowed on the stage.

6) Participants should perform a part of their dance for 2(+1) minutes in the PRELIMS round with/without costumes and props.
(Prelims round will be held if teams participating are more than 6).

7) Pre-recorded music should be brought in a CD in a standard audio format (.wma/.mp3) and a pen drive and should be submitted at least 30 minutes before the PRELIMS round.

8) Participants name, college name should be clearly mentioned on the CD.

9) Live music is not allowed.

10) The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

11) Judging criteria: Choreography, Costumes, Expressions, Co-ordination, Stage utilization, Overall impact.

RAZZMATAZZ

Group Dance

"Dance till the stars come down from the rafters Dance, Dance, Dance till you drop."
It is said that dancing is like dreaming with your feet. Dancing gives you those moments when you let yourself loose and let the passion from your soul sway your body with the rhythm. An excellent mix of intensity, synchronization, talent, and energy. Razzmatazz is back to rock the floor with the most talented dance groups out there. Get set go and redefine group dance in a way no one has seen before. Take the dance floor by storm with your passion and unleash the wildness within you at Vivacity 2018.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

1) The number of members Performing can be 6 to 20. A maximum number of helpers can be 4 (Helpers must also bring the college IDs).

2) Not less than 5 participants on stage at any time of the performance.

3) Time limit for the PRELIMS will be minimum 3 to maximum 5 minutes (including the setup time)

44) Time limit for the FINALS will be minimum 7 to maximum 15 minutes (including the setup time)

5) The time limit should be strictly adhered to. Teams shall be penalized by judges for not doing so.

6) Any kind of fluid or flame usage is not allowed on the stage.

7) Each team should bring 2 CD's of their final track in standard audio CD Format and their own USB drive which has to be submitted at least 6 hours before the start of the event.

8) Team name and college name must be clearly written on the CD.

9) The decision of the judges shall be final and binding.

10) All forms of dance are allowed.

11) Judging criteria: Choreography, Costumes, Expressions, Co-ordination, Stage utilization, Overall impact, creativity.

NOTE: Participants must have a valid college ID-card to participate. In case of any discrepancy, the team shall be disqualified.

PUMP IT UP

Solo Western Dance

All eyes are on you and you alone. Forget about synchronization and formation. Remember, it is always solo at the top. Be prepared. It's time to step it up a notch at VIVACITY-2018's solo dance competition. Spin and sway, prance and play-All the world is a stage and all stage is yours.

RULES FOR THE EVENT

GENERAL RULES

1) Individual participation.

2) The event comprises 3 rounds.

1ST ROUND

1) Prepared Sequence.

2) Time limit will minimum 1.5 to maximum 2 minutes (including the setup time)

3) All forms of dance allowed.

4) Fusion allowed (Classical dance forms not allowed).

5) The time limit should not be exceeded.

6) Get two copies of your song one in CD and one in pen drive for the dance.

NOTE: 1) Songs should be in .mp3 format.

2) No audio cassettes will be allowed.

7) The song should be reported one hour prior to the PRELIMS.

2nd ROUND

1) The short-listed participants from the 1st Round will be given some prop by us and will perform on the song of your choice (it should be available).

2) Time limit will minimum 1.5 to maximum 2 minutes (including the setup time)

3) The time limit should not be exceeded.

FINALS

1) The short-listed participants from round two have to perform on the songs given by us.

2) Time limit will minimum 1.5 to maximum 2 minutes (including the setup time)
